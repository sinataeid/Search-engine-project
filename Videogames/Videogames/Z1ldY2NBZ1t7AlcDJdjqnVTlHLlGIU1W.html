You Don't Need Instruments To Rock!

Great bands don’t just play their music, they perform it! Play and perform moves like a rock star as never before. All you need is your Wii Remote, Nunchuk, and a little bit of rhythm to become the ultimate “air band.” Get into character and show-off unique performance skills as the drummer, lead guitarist, bass guitarist, and frontman.
