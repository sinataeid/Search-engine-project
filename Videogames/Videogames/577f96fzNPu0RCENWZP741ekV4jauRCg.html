Game Overview

Take the stage like a chipmunk!

Rock out with the Chipmunks! Inspired by the #1 kids musical group of all time,

Alvin and The Chipmunks makes its debut on gaming systems for the first time

ever! Players will play as the Chipmunks as they rock their way to the top of the charts by winning the ultimate battle of the bands contest, Rockathonapalooza. Alvin and the Chipmunks features fun music gameplay, popular licensed songs spanning many different eras, and a variety of play modes including multiplayer and more. Get ready to rock until you drop with Alvin and The Chipmunks in a brand new music-inspired adventure!

  • First Alvin and the Chipmunks game ever.
  • Live out rock stardom dreams with the power trio of Alvin and The Chipmunks
  • Sing and play along to a soundtrack featuring popular songs from the past and present including smash hits such as 'All the Small Things', 'Love Shack', 'Walking on the Sun', 'Karma Chameleon', 'It's Tricky', 'Heartbreak Hotel' and MORE!
  • Includes the Chipmunk classic and pop culture favorite 'The Chipmunks Song.'
  • Comical storyline that incorporates the newly designed characters and their adventures.
  • Challenge friends thru multiplayer in Versus mode.
  • Various levels of difficulty will keep players of all ages engaged.
  • Family-friendly fun, easy enough for kids and parents to play together with characters and music that both will love!
