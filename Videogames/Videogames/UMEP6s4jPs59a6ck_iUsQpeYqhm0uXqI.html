Zoo Vet will tug on your heartstrings, make you laugh, and challenge your mind.

Test your skills as an Animal Doctor in this fun and educational vet simulation. Using your stylus, give check-ups, treat major and minor illnesses,and even perform surgeries. Emergencies sometimes arise and players must act fast or risk losing a life! Successfully treat all the animals to work your way up to being the most valuable vet at the zoo.
