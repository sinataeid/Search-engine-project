More

Visit your Uncle's Farm and meet over 18 different loveable animals!

Feed your new best friends, clean them, walk them, play with them and keep them happy to earn their love and loyalty. Featuring Yoneo Morita’s HanaDeka’s renowned (Big Nose) style of animal photography, adorable animal fanatics of all ages will love this exciting collection of 18 different furry friends. Take great photographs of your pet friends, collect the pictures and complete your photo album.
