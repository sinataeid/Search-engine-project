Use your Fantasy!

Use your Fantasy! PLÄTTCHEN – twist ’n’ paint is a crazy, challenging puzzle shooter with more than 100 hours of fun that supports up to eight players simultaneously. Guard the birth of ZeLeLi and free them from the menace of the evil black Shadow Plättchen. Play as one of the countless FantasyMEs and fight as an astronaut, butterfly, car, football, or many more against sharks, cowboys, asteroids, and skulls in numerous, fabulous Fantasy Worlds. Use items and multipliers in combos to fight your way to the top of the high scores and find the perfect way to solve a level! Go wild in the three completely different game modes of PLÄTTCHEN – twist ’n’ paint with more than 300 levels. Twist yourself along with your friends cooperatively or against each other through the many multiplayer modes.

Available now from the Wii Shop Channel
