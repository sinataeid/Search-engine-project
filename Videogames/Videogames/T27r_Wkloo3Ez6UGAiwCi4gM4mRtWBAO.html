Fishermen can hook to the limit with this unique Wii fishing game.

Cast your line and set your hook for fishing fun with the Core Gamer Wii Fishing Rod Controller. Packed with Aksys Wii tiel Hooked! Real Motion fishing. Universally created for your Nintendo Wii and compatible fishing game software. Enjoy great fishing action without ever leaving the comfort of your couch. The fully functional rod and rotating reel simulates a natural fishing experience. Don't let the big one get away.

You'll be working up a sweat as you lean your Wii Remote in every direction, all the while reeling on the Nunchuk to catch the big one with the included Fishing Rod. It's as if you have a rod and reel right in your hands! A wide range of fishing environments: Explore six photorealistic lakes, from a meteorite crater turned lake to a man-made recreational reservoir. Three exciting single-player gameplay modes: Hone your skills in Practice or Time Attack mode. Then take on Tournament mode to see if you can become the next world champion!
