As a new menace threatens the galaxy, Planetary Patrol officers Rallen and Jeena must excavate, awaken and evolve a new team of Spectrobes and take them into battle to save the universe from destruction.

FEAUTRES

  • Double the number of Spectrobes
  • All-new battle system - take on enemies in the field as Rallen and control 2 Spectrobes in epic battles against the Krawl
  • Enhanced search and excavation system - dig in rock, lava, ice and more
  • Play as Jeena on missions where her puzzle-solving skills are needed
  • Over 30 new Code Input Cards - use your cards from the original Spectrobes, too
