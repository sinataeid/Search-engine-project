Game Overview

Candy Land is under attack!

This once sweet and tasty land has been taken over by hordes of snapping Cup Cakes, Angry Bees and Jelly Monsters.

Only one man can stop this evil army of monster cakes. He’s one tough cookie! A guy that won’t crumble under the pressure…… ‘Ninjabread Man’ is here!

  • Fight your way through strange and dangerous levels to bring back that sweet taste of home back to Candy Land.
  • Learn advanced Ninja fighting techniques and build up your weapons to reduce enemies into a quivering pool of Raspberry Jam.
  • Ninjabread Man utilises the Wii control system in a fun and interactive way.
  • Throw Ninja stars to stun enemies and then follow up with Ninjabread Man's mighty samurai sword. Whatever direction you swing, so will Ninjabread Man.
  • Every level is filled with enemies bent on the pursuit of power!
  • Replay levels in challenge mode. Collecting the hidden objects and racing against the clock to unlock a special features. You’ll be sure to face some unexpected hazards along the way.
