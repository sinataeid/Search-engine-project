Challenge your family to Disney’s ultimate trivia showdown!

TH!NK You’re Fast? TH!NK Again! It’s the ultimate trivia showdown that puts your family to the test. Play as a classic Disney character in a game-show style quiz game hosted by the Genie. Prove your smarts with over 5,000 Disney trivia and general knowledge questions. Show off your speed in the mini-games and fastest-finger game rounds. It’s easy and fun to play for the entire family!

- Over 15 unique rounds of game play

- Be entertained by the Genie and special guests including Stitch, Timon, and Sebastian

- Fun for players of all ages with the unique visual answer system

- Play as a classic Disney character—Mickey, Minnie, Donald, Goofy and more

- Pick from four locations to set your game – Lilo and Stitch, Lion King and more

- Play with up to 4 people using Wii Remotes or a combination of Wii Remotes and Nunchuks

- Customize your game and pick your favorite rounds to play
