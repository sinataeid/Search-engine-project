From Chun Soft, comes a handheld role-playing game starring a fearless hero named Shiren the Wanderer and his trusty weasel companion, Koppa.

Quest for the legendary Lair of the Golden Condor, venture through treacherous lands and discover the mystery surrounding this mythical realm. Many warriors have set out on this grand adventure but none have survived the perilous journey through deep caverns, vast forests and dangerous mountain passes. The path is filled with ferocious monsters, hidden traps and thieves that Shiren and Koppa must battle through and survive in order to reach their goal and claim their prize.

  • Classic RPG Experience: Turn-based action gameplay allows you time to plan your attacks and keeps you on your toes with levels that are unpredictable in nature.
  • Randomly Generated Dungeons: New unexpected challenges continuously await the player as they enter each dungeon leading up to an addictive 30+ hour adventure experience.
  • Enhancements from Original Game: Upgrades include three expanded territories, multiple monsters, revamped graphics & sounds, and a brand new tutorial mode.

How does this game use Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection?

If you get defeated in a dungeon, summon another player to revive you via Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection.
