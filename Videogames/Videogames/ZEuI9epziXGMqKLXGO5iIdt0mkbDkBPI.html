Game Overview

Gameplay Images
More

AMF Bowling: Pinbusters! allows you to experience the excitement of the lanes!

Take on the challenge alone in one of the various single-player modes or strike up competition with your friends in multiplayer mode. Bowl Now allows for instant action! No bowling shoes required. Tournament Mode offers league bowling for serious competition. Practice Mode allows you to refine your technique and offers exciting mini games that are both challenging and entertaining.

Full Use of Wii Controller: With easy to learn controls that mimic real bowling, it's the next best thing to being at the lanes. Bright, Colorful Party Atmosphere: Mirrored balls, hot neon lights, and strobe effects…Pinbusters! offers unique environments and an atmosphere like no other. Unique Mini Games: Looking for a quick challenge or something a little different? Try your hand at a variety of unique challenges with Pool Bowl and Obstacle Course mini games. Eight Playable Characters: Choose from eight unique characters, such as the Surfer, the Princess, the Drill Sergeant, Punk Rock Girl, the Cowgirl, and the Jock - each with their own unique personality.
