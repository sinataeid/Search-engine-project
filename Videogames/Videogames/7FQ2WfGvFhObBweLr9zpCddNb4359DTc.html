Create, Guide, and Evolve your Unique Spore Creature.

Guide and evolve your custom creature on a quest to save your species and the entire Galaxy. Meet, befriend, or fight other creatures in a number of unique Touch Screen based activities. Evolve your creature's look and abilities by collecting and adding parts in the Creature Creator. Eventually uncover the mystery of the Galaxy's troubles and work to set things right!
