When Mommy and Daddy go out, their little angels turn into devils!

You’re hired for the ultimate teenage job of multi-tasking mania! Use the Touch Screen to clean up the house, cook, feed, and put the unruly kids to bed before their parents return home. It’s just like being a real babysitter - only a lot more fun!
