Become The Incredible Hulk and unleash his destructive power. This action/platforming game is available on the Nintendo DS in June 2008.

Cursed to contain the rampaging monster known as "The Hulk" within him,

scientist Bruce Banner is living in the shadows, scouring the planet for

an antidote to the gamma radiation infection that turns him into a

living engine of destruction. But the warmongers who dream of abusing

his powers won't leave him alone, nor will his need to be with the woman

he loves, Betty Ross.

  • Hulk smash your way through 30 levels!
  • Compete head-to-head with players via local wireless play.
