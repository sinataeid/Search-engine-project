Defeat Wendigo with puzzles and spells!

Brave must use quick thinking and magic spells to fight off the evils rising against him and his people. Defeat Wendigo and his 6 strongest shamans by matching combinations of elements in puzzles based in 6 unique worlds. Unlock new elemental spells as you travel on your quest, use them in battle, and even save yourself from the brink of death with the unique "Last Breath" feature.
