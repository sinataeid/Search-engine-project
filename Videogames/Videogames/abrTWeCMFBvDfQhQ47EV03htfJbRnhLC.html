Game Overview

Become a famous French chef!

Experience the thrilling life of a famous gourmet chef and master the art of French cooking. Enjoy fun and accessible missions where you cook for different customers and food critics, and ultimately become the top chef at a high-end French restaurant. In the process, you’ll learn more than 70 authentic gourmet recipes that you can bring to life in your own kitchen!

Features:

Fulfill your dream of becoming a famous gourmet chef

  • Earn the rank of master chef by perfecting French cuisine
  • Use the stylus to prepare, cut, mix and cook meals
  • Watch the kitchen come to life as you use cooking utensils and move effortlessly throughout the different work stations
  • Please more than 20 customers, including a food critic who comes in various disguises

Bring your gourmet mastery into your own kitchen

  • All recipes that you cook during the game are available in the recipe section
  • Use your Nintendo DS as a cookbook and show off your skills to friends and family
