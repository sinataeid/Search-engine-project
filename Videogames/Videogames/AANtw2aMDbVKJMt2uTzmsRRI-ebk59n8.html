More

Come and unleash your creativity

More than just a diary, it's about a game to express your personality while having fun. Not only can you keep your events in the interactive agenda, you can also:

  • express yourself in the diary, which is fully customizable like an interactive scrapbook
  • know yourself better with dozens of fun quizzes and your personality star
  • socialize with your friends trading tons of customization elements, sharing secrets, taking quizzes together or playing mini-games

A Fully Customizable Diary

  • Keep your secrets safe by locking your game with a secret code
  • Store up to 150 pages in your diary
  • Customize your pages with more than 500 items (backgrounds, photos, stickers, pins, etc) like an interactive scrapbook
  • More than 240 special pages where you are invited to write about specific topics in line with your profile and your mood
  • Note all your important events in the agenda

Design avatars that look like you and your friends

  • Use 500+ personalization items : gender, skin colour, hair, clothes, shoes, accessories; each available in several colors
  • Create very detailed cards (including nickname, avatar, hobbies) for you and your friends (save up to 30 friends cards)

Know yourself better

  • Take personality quizzes to discover your inner feelings about You & Yourself, You & the Others, You & the World
  • 48 fun quizzes : Path Test, Multi-Choice, Slide Test and Quick Test
  • Follow your personality profile through the personality star that changes according to your answers in the quizzes
  • Keep track of your mood of the day taking a picture of it

Socialize with your friends

Share DS mode (around your DS)

  • Invite 1 to 4 friends to have fun playing mini-games
  • Let your friends play to discover the secrets you marked for them
  • Take Group Quizzes with them and discover how close your are
  • Discover how deep your friends know you letting them guessing your answers in the Quizzes
  • Let your friends take the same quizzes you did and collect their profiles in the Friend Cards Local Wireless (2 players)
  • Create a page for a friend and send it as a gift
  • Trade items with your friends for your diary and avatars creations Voice chat over the internet (2 players)

Have fun playing mini games when alone

  • 3 solo mini-games
  • Discover the future breaking the Fortune Cookie

How does this game use Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection?

Talk with your friend wherever they are through Wi-Fi connection
