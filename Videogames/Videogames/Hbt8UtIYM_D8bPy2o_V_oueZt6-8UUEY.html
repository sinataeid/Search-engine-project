Game Overview

Serving up addictive head to head gameplay that’s fun for everyone.

 Rockstar Games presents Table Tennis for the Wii has got all of the

accessible pick up and playability, killer ball physics, and adrenaline-pumping table tennis action that made it a classic, while utilizing all of the the motion sensing interactive control technology of the Nintendo Wii to serve up addictive head to head gameplay that’s fun for everyone.

Supreme accessible pick-up and playability

Single Player, Head to Head, Exhibition or Tournament game play modes

Beautifully captured animations and expressions

Ultra realistic R.A.G.E. driven physics

11 players, each with different strengths and weaknesses

19 unique arenas

3 Control methods

Standard – Wii Remote only

Control Freak – Wii Remote & Nunchuk

Sharp Shooter – Wii Remote & Nunchuk

4 different combinable spins and power shots
