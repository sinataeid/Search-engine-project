The #1 best-selling new puzzle game of 2007 is back in an all new intergalactic adventure. Experience the incredibly addictive gameplay that revolutionized the RPG/Puzzle genre. Customize your ship, scour the galaxy for clues, and build your best team of puzzle fighters to uncover a dangerous plot that threatens the entire universe. Winner of IGN's Best DS Puzzle game of E3 2008 and nominated for the Game Critics Awards Best DS Game Overall of E3 2008.
