A seek and solve adventure that leads you in search of ancient Mayan ruins.

As a famed archaeologist, you have discovered a mysterious Mayan glyph that holds the key to a long-lost Mayan temple. You must use your sharp mind and eye for detail as you seek and solve your way to uncovering The Forgotten Ruins.
